Arrival and Departure from Taniti

A majority of visitors arrive and leave Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.

Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airpot so larger jets will be able to land on the island. This expansion will happen within the next few years.
