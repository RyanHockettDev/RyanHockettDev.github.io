Food and drink on Taniti

With an economy rich in agriculture and fishing, there is no shortage of food and beverage on the island.

Important notes on Alcohol sales: The drinking age is 18 on Taniti, but is not strictly enforced. Alcohol will not be served between the hours of midnight and 9:00 AM

The island is currently home to 10 restaurants.

5 serve local fish and rice

3 serve American-style meals

2 serve Pan-asian cuisine

Taniti also has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.
